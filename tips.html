Safety Tips

At Home

  • Keep emergency contact numbers saved and visible.
  • Secure doors and windows with strong locks and check them regularly.
  • Install a peephole or smart camera to monitor your door.
  • Teach kids and elders how to contact emergency services.

While Traveling

  • Share your route with trusted people.
  • Avoid isolated paths and dark areas, especially at night.
  • Keep your mobile charged and your location service enabled.
  • Be aware of surroundings and report suspicious activities.

Digital Safety

  • Enable emergency sharing apps or location trackers.
  • Don't overshare personal details on social media.
  • Install verified security apps and keep them updated.
  • Be cautious of scams, especially asking for OTPs or passwords.
